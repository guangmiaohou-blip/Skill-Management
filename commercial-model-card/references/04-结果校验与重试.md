# 结果校验与重试

输出前按优先级检查：

1. 模特身份是否匹配对应模特卡。
2. 身体比例、头肩比、脖子、肩宽、肢体长度是否稳定。
3. 强制身份标记是否保留。
4. 外观层是否只改变用户允许的内容。
5. 产品形状、尺寸、颜色、材质、组件、品牌决策和比例是否正确。
6. 手部、接触、握持、佩戴、操作、重量和方向是否可信。
7. 光源预设是否命中；皮肤高光、阴影、对比度和氛围是否符合要求，且没有改变肤色预设、肤质预设或产品颜色。
8. 场景、姿势、构图、镜头、画幅和输出渠道是否符合要求。
9. 如果是全身、七分身或远景图，面部是否仍能读出 C01 的三庭五眼、右眼眼皮痣、雀斑和关键肤质；人物是否占画面高度约 88%-92%，没有因远景留白导致面部信息丢失。
10. 如果有头巾、帽子、耳机等遮挡型头部造型，头部是否没有被放大；头巾/帽子是否贴合，耳机耳罩是否符合真实比例，头肩关系是否稳定。
11. 如果露出身体皮肤，是否保留弱化但可见的身体真实细节；手臂、肩膀、胸口是否避免大面积油光和平滑塑料感。
12. 如果颈部可见，颈部阴影是否柔和连接下巴、脖子、锁骨和肩部，且没有脏黑断层或完全抹平。
13. 若涉及侧脸、三分之二侧脸、回眸、局部回填、头饰/耳机、脸身肤色统一、耳位或下颌，是否通过 `models/c01/C01-独立头部结构强检测.md` 和 `models/c01/C01-局部回填与头部结构校验.md` 的专项检查。
14. 是否存在强检测一票否决项：下颌链不闭合、耳朵落在后脑、颞下颌关节无效、面部前平面展开、侧脸面积与旋转矛盾、面部到耳前距离异常、头部各结构姿态不一致、裸头骨无法闭合，或通过移动耳位/扩大后脑/抬高颅顶适配配件。任一出现即直接重试，不展示样张。

## 身份失败重试

```text
Regenerate with stronger model-card identity priority. Restore the original face shape, eye shape, nose, lips, body frame, head-shoulder ratio, body proportion, and mandatory identity marks. Keep the successful pose, camera, lighting, and composition unchanged. Do not inherit the reference person's identity.
```

## 全身图身份丢失重试

```text
Use the full-body two-stage identity workflow. Keep the successful full-body pose, wardrobe, body proportion, camera, and background. Re-anchor the face to the high-precision C01 face master: restore C01 original three-court/five-eye proportions, original eye spacing, forehead ratio, nose base, mouth position, chin length, right-eyelid mole, freckles, skin tone, skin texture, makeup, and lighting. Increase the figure scale to about 88%-92% of image height while keeping full head and feet visible. Do not preserve any small-face simplification, genericized face, or facial drift from the failed full-body output.
```

## 头饰、身体肤质或颈部阴影失败重试

```text
Keep C01 identity, face, pose, product, background, and composition unchanged. Correct only the accessory and body realism layer: restore C01 original head size and head-to-shoulder scale; make headwear thin and close-fitting; keep headphones proportional and not oversized. Add subtle body-real-skin details at 10%-20% strength on neck, collarbones, shoulders, arms, hands, and visible chest: mild skin tone variation, fine body pores, gentle muscle plane, faint veins, knuckle/tendon detail. Reduce oily shine on arms, shoulders, and chest. Restore soft neck structural shadow connecting jaw, neck, collarbones, and shoulders without dirty black patches or erased shadows.
```

## 下颌—耳位—侧脸面积联合重试

```text
Regenerate using C01 independent head-structure authority. Do not preserve the edit target's skull, jaw, ear position, side-face area, or facial projection unless they pass structural validation. Rebuild one coherent C01 bare skull from the official three-view reference. Keep a single consistent yaw, pitch, and roll across eyes, nose, mouth, chin, jaw, ears, cranial top, and occiput. Restore the continuous chin-to-mandibular-body-to-angle-to-ramus-to-TMJ chain. Place the ear on the lateral skull with its canal behind the ramus and the TMJ immediately anterior. Correct excessive far-eye visibility, expanded side-face area, stretched nose-to-ear distance, forward-pasted face, rearward ear, enlarged occiput, or raised crown. Preserve the successful body pose, shoulders, product, wardrobe, scene, lighting, crop, and composition. Add headwear or headphones only after the naked skull passes validation.
```

## 产品失败重试

```text
Keep the model identity unchanged. Restore the supplied product's exact geometry, unchanged color, materials, component placement, confirmed branding, and real scale. Correct the grip, contact, orientation, and operation without inventing accessories, ports, controls, text, or functions.
```

```text
Regenerate as a controlled preset reference image. Keep the same size, front-facing angle, head-and-shoulders crop, white background, lighting, expression, hairstyle, and wardrobe. Show the complete head and visible collar. Change only the selected preset variable.
```
