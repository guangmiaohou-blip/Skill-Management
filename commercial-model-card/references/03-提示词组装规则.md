# 提示词组装规则

按“身份锁定 → 外观层 → 产品规则 → 场景任务 → 排除项”的顺序组装。

```text
Use case: commercial model-card identity preserve.

Model card: [C01].

Input classification:
- Image A: mandatory model identity.
- Image B: model body or wardrobe reference.
- Image C: expression, angle, or comparison reference.
- Image D: product identity, detail, branding, or scale reference.
- Image E: form-only pose, scene, camera, or lighting reference.

Priority: model identity and body consistency; product fidelity; believable model-product interaction; requested scene, pose, and style; commercial composition and selling-point emphasis.

Identity lock: [load from C01-身份锁定.md].

Facial proportion lock: Preserve C01's original three-court and five-eye facial proportions from the identity reference. Keep the original forehead height, brow-eye height, eye spacing, nose base position, mouth position, chin length, and face width. Do not beautify, stretch, compress, center-shift, or rebalance the face into a different generic beauty proportion.

Appearance layer:
- Skin tone preset: [warm-ivory by default, soft-honey-beige, deep-amber-bronze, or custom].
- Texture profile: [natural-real, dewy-beauty, matte-technical, true-real-skin, or custom].
- Lighting preset: [soft-even-studio by default, premium-softbox, outdoor-diffused-light, natural-soft-proof-light, or custom].
- Makeup, hairstyle, wardrobe, expression: [user request].
- Micro-features: [right-eyelid mole mandatory; freckles optional].

Reference comparison rule: [only when generating preset comparison images].

Product invariants: [shape, size, color, material, component layout, branding decision, real scale].

Form-only rule: reference image is form-only; model identity is mandatory. Extract only pose, camera, framing, scene, and lighting. Do not inherit the reference person's identity.

Lighting rule: Apply the selected lighting preset only to highlight strength, shadow softness, contrast, direction, and atmosphere. Do not change model identity, three-court/five-eye facial proportions, selected skin tone, selected skin texture, product color, or product material truth.

True-real-skin rule: When using `true-real-skin`, explicitly request low retouching, visible fine pores, slight uneven tone, natural lip texture, faint under-eye texture, and tiny skin details. Pair with `natural-soft-proof-light` and `bare-natural-makeup` unless the user specifies otherwise. Exclude poreless skin, glass skin, dewy glow, satin glow, plastic skin, strong highlighter, glossy lips, forehead hotspot, nose-bridge glare, cheek oil shine, and overexposure.

Local-fill/head-structure rule: For face/head inpainting, headwear or headphones, skin-tone transfer, ear placement, or jaw correction, insert only the applicable constraints from `models/c01/C01-局部回填与头部结构校验.md`.

Independent head-structure rule: For profile, three-quarter profile, over-the-shoulder, face/head inpainting, headwear, headphones, ear placement, or jaw correction, load `models/c01/C01-独立头部结构强检测.md`. Do not treat the edit target or an external AI reference as skull, jaw, ear-position, side-face-area, or facial-projection authority until it passes validation. Use the official C01 three-view as independent structural authority. Estimate one coherent yaw, pitch, and roll for the entire skull. Enforce the continuous chin-to-mandibular-body-to-angle-to-ramus-to-TMJ chain; place the ear canal behind the ramus with the TMJ immediately anterior; keep the ear on the lateral skull. Check far-eye visibility, far-cheek visibility, side-face projection area, and nose/eye/mouth/chin-to-TMJ relationships. Reject forward-pasted face, expanded side-face area, rearward ear, stretched face-to-ear distance, enlarged occiput, raised crown, or a naked skull that cannot close. Add headwear or headphones only after the naked skull passes.

Headwear/headphone occlusion rule: When using bandanas, hats, headbands, over-ear headphones, or any accessory that covers the hairline, skull top, ears, or side head, treat it as an appearance layer on top of C01's original head. Preserve C01's true head height, cranial top, face length, chin ratio, face width, and head-to-shoulder scale. Keep fabric thin and close-fitting unless the user requests otherwise. Keep the combined headwear + headphone silhouette from enlarging the visual head volume by more than about 5%-8%. Do not let accessories redefine skull size, compress the face, or make earcups oversized.

Body-real-skin-detail rule: When neck, collarbones, shoulders, arms, hands, chest, waist, or abdomen are visible, separate body skin from face skin. Keep body details subtle but present at about 10%-20% strength: collarbone shadow, shoulder-neck transition, gentle upper-arm muscle plane, very faint subcutaneous veins on hands/forearms/collarbone area, mild body skin tone variation, tiny body pores, knuckle/tendon/wrist/finger joint detail. Body details must support realism without stealing focus from the face. Exclude flat plastic body skin, oily arms, glossy chest, medical veins, excessive boniness, exaggerated muscles, or rough aged texture.

Neck structural shadow rule: When jaw, neck, and collarbones are visible, preserve a soft natural shadow from chin to neck and under the jaw to connect head, neck, collarbones, and shoulders. Use low-to-medium intensity with feathered edges. Do not erase the shadow completely, and do not create dirty-neck shadows, hard black cutouts, or disconnected neck shading.

Full-body two-stage identity rule: For full-body, seven-eighths, distant, or small-face compositions, do not rely on a single full-body generation to preserve C01 identity. First use or create a high-precision face master that locks C01 face, three-court/five-eye proportions, right-eyelid mole, freckles, skin tone, skin texture, makeup, and lighting. Then generate the full-body image using official C01 identity/body references plus the face master. Treat the face master as identity and appearance authority only; do not inherit its crop, close-up composition, perspective, or any facial-proportion drift. Keep the final figure at about 88%-92% of image height while still showing full head and feet.

Task: [scene, pose, expression, camera, lighting preset, crop, aspect ratio].

Commercial brief: [product category, output channel, selling point, required product face, interaction, occlusion limits, branding decision, approved effect enhancement].

Exclude: identity drift, three-court/five-eye proportion drift, facial-geometry drift, inconsistent head yaw/pitch/roll, expanded side-face area, excessive far-eye or far-cheek visibility, forward-pasted face, stretched nose-to-ear distance, broken jaw chain, rearward ear, ear on the occiput, invalid TMJ, enlarged occiput, raised crown, naked-skull closure failure, changed eye spacing, shifted eye height, changed forehead ratio, shortened or lengthened chin, enlarged head from headwear, helmet-like bandana, oversized earcups, body-frame drift, uncontrolled oily skin shine, glossy arms, flat plastic body skin, missing collarbone/neck/arm structure, dirty neck shadow, erased neck shadow, overexposed forehead, harsh nose glare, glossy lips, glass skin, poreless skin, dewy glow, satin glow, product color shift, invented accessories, invented text, distorted hands, extra fingers, plastic skin, anime styling, watermarks.
```

对参考图或外观预设对比图，必须明确“只改变当前比较变量”。
