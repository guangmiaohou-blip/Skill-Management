# 提示词组装规则

按“身份锁定 → 外观层 → 产品规则 → 场景任务 → 排除项”的顺序组装。

```text
Use case: commercial model-card identity preserve.

Model card: [C01 or C02; use exactly one and never mix identities].

Input classification:
- Image A: mandatory model identity.
- Image B: model body or wardrobe reference.
- Image C: expression, angle, or comparison reference.
- Image D: product identity, detail, branding, or scale reference.
- Image E: form-only pose, scene, camera, or lighting reference.

Priority: model identity and body consistency; product fidelity; believable model-product interaction; requested scene, pose, and style; commercial composition and selling-point emphasis.

Identity lock: [load from the selected model card's 身份锁定.md].

Full-body proportion lock: [load all target values and anatomical measurement boundaries from the selected model card's 比例控制.md]. Never hardcode, average, or inherit another model card's values. Measure anatomical vertical lengths rather than clothing waistlines, hip points, crops, hairstyle height, or footwear.

Facial proportion lock: Preserve the selected model card's original three-court and five-eye facial proportions from its identity reference. Keep the original forehead height, brow-eye height, eye spacing, nose base position, mouth position, chin length, and face width. Do not beautify, stretch, compress, center-shift, or rebalance the face into a different generic beauty proportion.

Appearance layer:
- Skin tone preset: [warm-ivory by default, soft-honey-beige, deep-amber-bronze, or custom].
- Texture profile: [natural-real, dewy-beauty, matte-technical, true-real-skin, or custom].
- Lighting preset: [soft-even-studio by default, premium-softbox, outdoor-diffused-light, natural-soft-proof-light, or custom].
- Makeup, hairstyle, wardrobe, expression: [user request].
- Micro-features: [load mandatory and optional micro-features from the selected model card's identity and appearance files].

Reference comparison rule: [only when generating preset comparison images].

Product invariants: [shape, size, color, material, component layout, branding decision, real scale].

Form-only rule: reference image is form-only; model identity is mandatory. Extract only pose, camera, framing, scene, and lighting. Do not inherit the reference person's identity.

Lighting rule: Apply the selected lighting preset only to highlight strength, shadow softness, contrast, direction, and atmosphere. Do not change model identity, three-court/five-eye facial proportions, selected skin tone, selected skin texture, product color, or product material truth.

True-real-skin rule: When using `true-real-skin`, explicitly request low retouching, visible fine pores, slight uneven tone, natural lip texture, faint under-eye texture, and tiny skin details. Pair with `natural-soft-proof-light` and `bare-natural-makeup` unless the user specifies otherwise. Exclude poreless skin, glass skin, dewy glow, satin glow, plastic skin, strong highlighter, glossy lips, forehead hotspot, nose-bridge glare, cheek oil shine, and overexposure.

Local-fill/head-structure rule: For face/head inpainting, headwear or headphones, skin-tone transfer, ear placement, or jaw correction, insert only the applicable constraints from the selected model card's `[current model]-局部回填与头部结构校验.md`.

Independent head-structure rule: For profile, three-quarter profile, over-the-shoulder, face/head inpainting, headwear, headphones, ear placement, or jaw correction, load the selected model card's `[current model]-独立头部结构强检测.md`. Apply the shared structural method using only the current role's official assets, parameters, and thresholds. Never use another role's skull, jaw, ear, side-face, or facial-projection values.

Face-detail refill rule: When the user requests face-detail refill, load only the selected model's `面部细节局部回填.md`. Refill complete facial identity, facial geometry, skin texture, freckles, lips, nose, brows, and mandatory identity marks at normal facial-detail fidelity. Preserve eye shape, eye spacing, eyelid opening, gaze, and existing ordinary eye appearance, but do not load eye-macro assets and do not generate iris, cornea, tear-film, eyelid-margin, or individual-lash microstructure.

Explicit-only eye-macro refill rule: Do not load or execute `眼部微距局部回填.md` unless the user explicitly names the eyes or both eyes and requests eye-macro refill, eye crop-enlarge refinement, or the eye-refinement module. Generic mentions of face refill, face-detail refill, crop, enlargement, local generation, refill, or compositing are insufficient. When invoked, run it only after face-detail refill has completed and passed validation. Use a new eye-only crop, eye-only references, eye-only masks, eye-only validation, and eye-only retry. Never combine face and eye refill in one generation call, crop, mask, prompt, validation pass, or retry.

Body-real-skin-detail rule: When neck, collarbones, shoulders, arms, hands, chest, waist, or abdomen are visible, separate body skin from face skin. Keep body details subtle but present at about 10%-20% strength: collarbone shadow, shoulder-neck transition, gentle upper-arm muscle plane, very faint subcutaneous veins on hands/forearms/collarbone area, mild body skin tone variation, tiny body pores, knuckle/tendon/wrist/finger joint detail. Body details must support realism without stealing focus from the face. Exclude flat plastic body skin, oily arms, glossy chest, medical veins, excessive boniness, exaggerated muscles, or rough aged texture.

Neck structural shadow rule: When jaw, neck, and collarbones are visible, preserve a soft natural shadow from chin to neck and under the jaw to connect head, neck, collarbones, and shoulders. Use low-to-medium intensity with feathered edges. Do not erase the shadow completely, and do not create dirty-neck shadows, hard black cutouts, or disconnected neck shading.

Full-body two-stage identity rule: For full-body, seven-eighths, distant, or small-face compositions, build a high-precision face master from the selected role's files, then generate the full body with that same role's identity, body, proportion, and wardrobe references. Treat the face master only as identity and appearance authority. Read all features, ratios, mandatory marks, and framing targets from the current role's files.

Task: [scene, pose, expression, camera, lighting preset, crop, aspect ratio].

Commercial brief: [product category, output channel, selling point, required product face, interaction, occlusion limits, branding decision, approved effect enhancement].

Exclude: identity drift, selected-role facial-proportion drift, facial-geometry drift, selected-role body-ratio drift, using another role's values or anatomical boundaries, treating clothing waistline or hip point as an anatomical boundary, inconsistent head yaw/pitch/roll, invalid skull/jaw/ear relationships, body-frame drift, uncontrolled oily skin shine, flat plastic body skin, missing body structure, product color shift, invented accessories, invented text, distorted hands, extra fingers, anime styling, watermarks. Add any role-specific exclusions from the selected model card's files.
```

对参考图或外观预设对比图，必须明确“只改变当前比较变量”。
