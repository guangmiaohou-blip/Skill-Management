# UGREEN 绿联电商视觉设计执行提示

## 使用方式

读取本目录的 `案例规范.md`，根据任务类型选择电商主图、详情页、官网 Banner、产品精修或设计审查流程。执行前确认产品、平台尺寸、主卖点、品牌资产授权和是否保留参考图中的文字或 Logo。

## 产品出图基础提示词

```text
UGREEN commercial e-commerce product visual, exact product proportions, no distortion, accurate interface layout, accurate logo placement only when explicitly authorized, three-quarter view, slight overhead angle, 20-degree tilt, commercial catalog quality, industrial precision, pixel-sharp, surface texture visible, material details accurate, daylight 5500K white balance, neutral color, pure white background when required, no hard shadow, clean hierarchy, product occupies 60 to 70 percent of the frame
```

## 负面提示词

```text
distorted product, wrong proportions, misplaced interface, warped logo, unreadable text, unauthorized brand mark, harsh shadow, warm or cool color cast, standard side view, cluttered layout, more than three text layers, plastic material, low resolution, compression artifacts, watermark
```

## 输出要求

输出正向提示词、负面提示词、推荐尺寸、品牌资产保留确认、产品结构检查和最终质量检查结果。参考图中的文字、Logo、图形和产品默认按总框架规则处理，不得自动保留。
